Rope play blends craftsmanship with safety. Choose beginner-friendly rope and learn how to keep your partner comfortable and secure.

  • Use smooth, soft rope that is easy to handle and release.
  • Avoid placing rope directly over nerves, joints, or sensitive areas.
  • Always have safety scissors nearby and a plan for release.

Learn slowly, practice knots, and never use rope without consent and careful monitoring.
