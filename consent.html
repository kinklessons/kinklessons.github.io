Consent

The cornerstone of healthy kink, centered on clear communication, choice, and the ability to stop or adjust at any time.

Home Kink 101 Bondage Rope Impact D/s

Consent means open dialogue, enthusiasm, and ongoing check-ins. It is safer and more respectful than assuming anything.

  • Talk through interests, boundaries, and hard limits before a scene.
  • Choose a safe word, safe signal, or both.
  • Respect that consent can change at any moment for any reason.

Healthy kink always starts with mutual agreement and honest listening.

Created as an introductory resource. Always prioritize communication, consent, and safety.
