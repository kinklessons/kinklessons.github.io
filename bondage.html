Bondage can be simple and gentle or more complex. Beginners should focus on comfort, communication, and basic safety.

  • Begin with soft restraints and easy positions.
  • Keep circulation, breathing, and mobility in mind.
  • Check in regularly and be ready to release quickly.

Practice with respect for each other’s boundaries and never rush into more advanced techniques.

Created as an introductory resource. Always prioritize communication, consent, and safety.
