Impact play uses intentional touch to create sensation. Start light, learn anatomy, and always check in with your partner.

  • Identify surface areas and avoid deep-tissue or nerve-rich zones.
  • Begin with gentle contact and adjust intensity slowly.
  • Offer aftercare and discuss how the experience felt afterwards.

Impact play is safest when both people feel informed, comfortable, and supported.

Created as an introductory resource. Always prioritize communication, consent, and safety.
