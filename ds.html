D/s

Dominance and submission can be a scene or a dynamic. Healthy D/s is rooted in negotiated power, trust, and consent.

D/s is about power exchange with agreed limits and responsibilities. It works best when both people share expectations clearly.

  • Talk about what dominance and submission mean to each person.
  • Build rituals, check-ins, and mutual rules that feel safe and meaningful.
  • Keep consent active and remember that any role can change.

Healthy D/s supports respect, boundaries, and ongoing communication.
